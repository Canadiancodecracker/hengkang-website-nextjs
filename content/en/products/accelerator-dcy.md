## Product Overview

Hengkang produces specialty accelerators for rubber vulcanization, leveraging our expertise in guanidine and cyanamide chemistry. These accelerators improve the curing process and final properties of rubber products.

## Product Range

### Guanidine-based Accelerators
- DPG (Diphenyl Guanidine)
- DOTG (Di-o-tolyl Guanidine)

### Thiourea Derivatives
- ETU (Ethylene Thiourea)
- DBTU (Dibutyl Thiourea)

## Applications

- Tire manufacturing
- Industrial rubber products
- Rubber hoses and belts
- Seals and gaskets
- Footwear
