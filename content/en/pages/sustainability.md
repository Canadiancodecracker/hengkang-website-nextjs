## Our Sustainability Commitment

At Hengkang, sustainability is integral to our business strategy. We are committed to responsible manufacturing practices that protect the environment, ensure employee safety, and create long-term value for all stakeholders.

## Environmental Protection (环境保护)

### Wastewater Treatment
- Advanced wastewater treatment systems
- Real-time water quality monitoring
- Compliance with national discharge standards
- Water recycling and conservation initiatives

### Air Emissions Control
- Modern emission control equipment
- Continuous emissions monitoring
- Volatile organic compound (VOC) reduction
- Dust control and filtration systems

### Waste Management
- Proper hazardous waste handling
- Recycling and resource recovery programs
- Waste minimization at source
- Licensed disposal partnerships

### Energy Efficiency
- Energy monitoring and optimization
- Heat recovery systems
- LED lighting and efficient equipment
- Renewable energy exploration

## Safety Production (安全生产)

### Safety Management System
- Provincial Safety Production Standardization Level 2 Enterprise
- Comprehensive safety policies and procedures
- Regular safety training for all employees
- Emergency response planning and drills

### Process Safety
- HAZOP studies for all processes
- Safety instrumented systems (SIS)
- Regular equipment inspection and maintenance
- Incident investigation and learning

### Occupational Health
- Personal protective equipment (PPE) programs
- Industrial hygiene monitoring
- Health surveillance for employees
- Ergonomic workplace design

### Fire Prevention
- Fire detection and suppression systems
- Regular fire drills and training
- Fire safety inspections
- Emergency evacuation procedures

## Green Chemistry Initiatives

We are actively implementing green chemistry principles:

1. **Atom Economy**: Maximizing incorporation of starting materials
2. **Safer Solvents**: Reducing use of hazardous solvents
3. **Energy Efficiency**: Optimizing reaction conditions
4. **Waste Prevention**: Designing processes to minimize waste
5. **Renewable Feedstocks**: Exploring bio-based alternatives

## Community Engagement

We are committed to being a responsible corporate citizen:

- Local employment and economic development
- Environmental education programs
- Community safety awareness initiatives
- Transparent communication with stakeholders

## Sustainability Goals

### Short-term (2024-2025)
- 10% reduction in water consumption per ton of product
- 100% compliance with emission standards
- Zero major safety incidents
- Enhanced digital monitoring systems

### Medium-term (2025-2028)
- 20% improvement in energy efficiency
- Carbon footprint assessment and reduction plan
- Expanded recycling programs
- Third-party sustainability audit

### Long-term (2028+)
- Carbon neutrality roadmap
- Circular economy integration
- Industry leadership in green manufacturing
- Sustainable supply chain partnerships

## Reporting & Transparency

We are committed to transparent reporting on our sustainability performance:

- Regular environmental monitoring data
- Safety statistics and incident reports
- Third-party audit reports
- Stakeholder communication
