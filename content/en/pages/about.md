## Company Overview

**Ningxia Hengkang Technology Co., Ltd.** was established in 2013 in Helan Industrial Park, Yinchuan, Ningxia, China. With a facility spanning **130,000 square meters** and registered capital of **¥200 million**, we are a leading high-tech enterprise specializing in guanidine-based intermediates, pharmaceutical APIs, animal nutrition, food additives, and advanced materials.

## Our Capabilities

Leveraging abundant talent resources and advanced equipment, we have undertaken multiple provincial-level R&D projects and achieved breakthrough innovations in:

- **Metformin Hydrochloride** production technology
- **Guanidineacetic Acid (GAA)** synthesis processes
- **Creatine** manufacturing methods
- **Ultrafine DICY** grinding technology

Our technical indicators have reached international advanced levels in these areas.
