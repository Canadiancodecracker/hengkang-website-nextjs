## Innovation Philosophy

Hengkang is committed to continuous innovation, leveraging our expertise in cyanamide chemistry to develop cutting-edge products and processes. Our R&D initiatives focus on:

- Process optimization and yield improvement
- Green chemistry and sustainable manufacturing
- New product development and applications
- Quality enhancement and cost reduction

## Research & Development Centers

### Ningxia Cyanamide Pharmaceutical Intermediate Engineering Research Center
Focused on developing new pharmaceutical intermediates and optimizing synthesis routes for APIs like Metformin Hydrochloride.

### Ningxia Guanidine Antidiabetic Engineering Technology Research Center
Dedicated to advancing guanidine-based diabetes treatment APIs and related synthesis technologies.

### Ningxia Pharmaceutical Intermediate (Helan) Technology Innovation Center
A regional innovation hub for pharmaceutical intermediate development and technology transfer.

### Yinchuan Technology Innovation Team
City-level recognition for our cyanamide pharmaceutical chemical synthesis innovation team.

## Key Technical Achievements

### Metformin Hydrochloride Production Technology
- World's largest single-line production facility (12,500 t/year)
- Advanced process control and automation
- Reduced environmental impact through green chemistry

### Guanidineacetic Acid (GAA) Synthesis
- High-yield synthetic process
- EU registered feed additive
- Consistent quality batch to batch

### Creatine Manufacturing Process
- 15,000 t/year production capacity
- Multiple grades and specifications
- Optimized crystallization technology

### Ultrafine DICY Technology
- Proprietary micronization process
- Particle size control (D50 3-5μm)
- Enhanced dispersibility and performance
