## About Ningxia Hengkang Technology

Founded in 2013, Ningxia Hengkang Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise integrating R&D, production, and sales of guanidine-based intermediates, pharmaceutical APIs, animal nutrition ingredients, food additives, and advanced materials.

With a 130,000 m² facility and ¥200 million registered capital, we leverage regional advantages in cyanamide chemistry to build a world-class green manufacturing base.
